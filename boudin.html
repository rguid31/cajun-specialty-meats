Boudin

Our traditional Louisiana boudin is a beloved Cajun delicacy made with seasoned pork, rice, and aromatics, all encased in a natural casing. This is comfort food at its finest - the taste of authentic Louisiana.

Features:

  • Traditional Louisiana recipe with pork and rice
  • Perfectly seasoned with Cajun spices
  • Natural casing
  • Ready to steam, grill, or bake
  • Made fresh daily

Serving Suggestions:

  • Steamed and eaten straight from the casing
  • Grilled for added smoky flavor
  • Served as appetizers at gatherings
  • Crumbled into dishes for extra flavor
  • Breakfast alongside eggs

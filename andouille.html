Andouille Sausage

Our authentic Louisiana-style andouille sausage is double-smoked to perfection, creating a bold, spicy flavor that's essential for traditional Cajun cooking. Made with premium pork and a proprietary blend of spices passed down through generations.

Features:

  • Double-smoked for authentic flavor
  • Traditional Louisiana recipe
  • Perfect for gumbo, jambalaya, and grilling
  • Made fresh in small batches
  • No artificial preservatives

Serving Suggestions:

  • Classic chicken and sausage gumbo
  • Jambalaya
  • Red beans and rice
  • Grilled and served with mustard
  • Cajun pasta dishes

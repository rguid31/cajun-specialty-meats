Little Rock's Cajun Food Festival This Weekend!

Mark your calendars! Little Rock's premier Cajun Food Festival is happening this Saturday and Sunday at Riverfront Park. This annual celebration of Louisiana culture brings the best of Cajun cuisine, live music, and family entertainment right here to Arkansas.

Down the Bayou Will Be There!

We're excited to announce that Down the Bayou will have a booth at the festival! Come visit us to sample and purchase our famous andouille sausage, boudin, and tasso ham. We'll be offering special festival pricing and showcasing some new products we've been working on.

Our team will be on hand to answer questions about Cajun cooking, share recipes, and help you select the perfect meats for your next Louisiana-inspired meal. Whether you're a longtime customer or new to Cajun cuisine, we'd love to see you there!

What to Expect at the Festival

This year's festival is shaping up to be the biggest yet! Here's what you can look forward to:

Authentic Cajun Food

Over 20 food vendors will be serving up Louisiana favorites including gumbo, jambalaya, po'boys, crawfish étouffée, beignets, and more. It's a chance to taste authentic Cajun cooking from some of the best chefs in the region. And of course, you'll find us serving up fresh-grilled andouille sausage and boudin!

Live Zydeco Music

The festival will feature live performances throughout both days. Saturday's headliner is the acclaimed Zydeco band "Bayou Groove" from Lafayette, Louisiana, bringing that authentic Cajun sound. Sunday features local favorite "The River City Cajuns" along with traditional accordion players and fiddlers.

Kids Activities

The festival is family-friendly with a dedicated kids' zone featuring face painting, bounce houses, and hands-on activities teaching children about Louisiana culture and wildlife. There's even a mini cooking demonstration area where kids can learn to make their own seasoning blends!

Cooking Demonstrations

Throughout the weekend, local chefs will be hosting live cooking demonstrations on the main stage. Learn how to make perfect jambalaya, master the art of the roux, and discover the secrets to authentic Cajun seasoning. These demonstrations are free with festival admission!

Festival Details

When: Saturday, October 19th (11 AM - 9 PM) and Sunday, October 20th (12 PM - 7 PM)

Where: Riverfront Park, Little Rock, AR

Admission: $10 for adults, $5 for children 12 and under, free for children under 5

Parking: Free parking available at nearby lots with shuttle service to the festival

Special Festival Deals

Stop by the Down the Bayou booth for exclusive festival specials! We'll be offering bundle deals on our most popular products and giving away free recipe cards with every purchase. Plus, sign up for our mailing list at the festival to receive a special discount code for your next in-store visit.

Planning Your Visit

The festival gets crowded, especially during meal times and evening concerts. We recommend arriving early if you want to avoid the biggest crowds. Bring cash for faster transactions, though most vendors do accept cards. And don't forget comfortable shoes - you'll be doing a lot of walking!

Weather forecast looks perfect for the weekend, with sunny skies and temperatures in the mid-70s. There will be shaded areas and misting stations to keep everyone cool.

See You at the Festival!

Visit the Down the Bayou booth at Little Rock's Cajun Food Festival. Can't make it? Stop by our store anytime!

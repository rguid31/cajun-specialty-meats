Everything You Need to Know About Our Cajun Meats

What makes your meats authentic Louisiana Cajun?

Our meats are prepared using traditional Louisiana recipes passed down through generations. We use the same spice blends, preparation methods, and smoking techniques that have been used in Cajun country for centuries. Our founder grew up in Louisiana learning these techniques from family butchers and pit masters.

How should I store andouille sausage?

Fresh andouille should be refrigerated and used within 5-7 days. For longer storage, you can freeze it for up to 3 months. Wrap it tightly in freezer paper or vacuum-seal bags to prevent freezer burn. Thaw in the refrigerator before use.

What's the best way to cook boudin?

Boudin can be steamed, grilled, or baked. For steaming, heat for about 10-15 minutes until warmed through. For grilling, cook over medium heat for 5-7 minutes per side. Many people eat it straight from the casing by squeezing it out - that's the traditional Louisiana way!

Are your products gluten-free?

Most of our products are naturally gluten-free, but we always recommend checking with our staff about specific items as some seasonings may contain gluten. We're happy to discuss ingredients for any dietary concerns.

How spicy are your products?

Our products have authentic Cajun seasoning, which provides bold flavor with moderate heat. They're flavorful without being overwhelmingly spicy. If you're sensitive to heat, our tasso is the spiciest, while our boudin is milder. We're happy to provide samples in-store!

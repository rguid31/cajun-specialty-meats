About Us

Bringing Louisiana Tradition to Arkansas

Our Story

Welcome to Down the Bayou Cajun Specialty Meats and Gifts, where the authentic flavors of Louisiana's bayou country come alive right here in Springdale, Arkansas. We are a retail store with in-house made fresh and frozen value-added meat products along with seasonings, gifts, decor, kitchen accessories and cookware. With over 200 specialty products, we serve a diverse audience seeking authentic Cajun flavors.

Our contact person Shane Acosta brings passion and expertise to serving our diverse audience with authentic Cajun flavors. After moving to Arkansas, we noticed a gap in the market for truly authentic Cajun meats - the kind that transport you straight to a Louisiana kitchen or a backyard crawfish boil.

We opened Down the Bayou to bring those authentic tastes to Arkansas. Every product we make follows time-honored recipes passed down through generations. From our signature andouille sausage to our perfectly seasoned boudin, we use only the finest ingredients and traditional preparation methods.

Our Mission

Authenticity

We're committed to preserving traditional Louisiana Cajun recipes and preparation methods, ensuring every product tastes just like it came from the bayou.

Quality

We source only the finest ingredients and craft our products in small batches to guarantee exceptional flavor and freshness in every bite.

Community

We're proud to be part of the Springdale community, sharing our Louisiana heritage and creating connections through great food.
